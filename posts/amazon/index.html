Frameworks / Mental Models

These are meta-ideas that I rely to make a sense of things, specially organisations. - Commandos, Infantry & Police - Amazon is not a Corporation, it is a collection of specialized units ranging from commandos, infantry to police. They don’t just conquer (as Snapchat did), or only cannabalize (as Facebook Inc. does), but they go all the way.
